Privacy Policy

SMS Filter

SMS Filter cleans up junk or spam SMSs by using a bunch or curated spam keywords across different categories. You can add your own block list of keywords or phone numbers to filter spam. If any SMSs have to be always allowed you can add them to the whitelist as well.

SMS Filter is private and cannot read, store or send any SMS over the network. All SMSs are filtered locally on your device using blocklist or whitelist keywords.

SMS Filter curates most-used spam keywords across different categories and uses that to filter spam or junk SMSs. These keywords will be constantly updated to identify spam messages quickly.
