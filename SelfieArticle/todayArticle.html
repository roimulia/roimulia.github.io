About Selfies. What Else?

by Christine R. Brown A+ a-



Selfies! The hot-topic of the moment and an activity which has influenced millions of people across the world. These days it seems, if it cant be photographed or videod, to upload to our social media sites its not worth doing! There are accessories available on the market to assist you in making better selfies and weve all seen the extending handles used by tourists everywhere(Anyone said Selfie Stick?)


Some emerging facts are making the news recently, as more young people look for an added edge to their postings and extreme selfies are becoming hugely popular. In Russia alone this year, over a dozen extreme selfie takers have been killed in the pursuit of their hobby. Some selfies could be your last so adrenaline junkies - have a care!

Most of us however, are content to take our selfies whilst doing the everyday things in our lives, as well adding a little interest with expression or humour, or even our pets! This is not an easy task if you have ever tried to take a selfie of you and your cat or dog together the struggle is real. We came across this new(and Free) app called Selfie Stream in the App Store and felt compelled to share it with you.

 "Taking selfies 10 times faster with 'Selfie stream" is the claim but i also found that taking selfies with Selfie Stream was ten times easier too! ºEditor Notesº  

Selfie Stream is an app dedicated to the Selfie and making the creation of these personal photographs easier and far better than ever before. With this ground-breaking new app, you can now set a timer, for continuous photo capture and take some awesome pics! Including stylish live filters & masks to perfect the image! be the first of your friends to put together a killer, pro selfie with this new trend!
